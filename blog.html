To become a full member of the association, a person must have attained the basic minimum qualification of a degree or its equivalent in Human Nutrition from a tertiary institution in Nigeria or abroad. A person shall be considered an Associate member if he/she is professionally engaged in related disciplines to nutrition namely; physiology, biochemistry, medicine, Agriculture, Food technology, Home Economics and Public health. A person must be inducted during the Annual General Meeting and Scientific Conference after screening, approval by Council and payment of requisite fees. Every member also has a unique membership ID.
